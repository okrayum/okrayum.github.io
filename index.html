City Services - Roadside Animal Reporting

Were here to provide safety, clean-up, and peace of mind.

It's a sad occurrence but animals often do get hit by cars, especially in high traffic areas. When this happens, there are often unseen hazards. The animal may attract disease and pests to your area, it may draw other animals that may also be in danger of traffic, also if just left in the street, that is poor treatment of the animal.

We make it real simple to report the location of an animal needing to be picked up. You have 3 choices:

  • Call out toll free number 1-888-888-5555 and leave a message with the adress and location, the type of animal, a brief description and if located in the street please mention if the middle or side of the road.
  • Use the reporting form on the next page and provide the listed information with as much detail as possible.
  • Use the map option to select the area where the animal is and drop a pin. Then select the description from the choices available and submit.

All pick-ups will be done the same day within 90 minutes. All animals are handled with care and laid to rest respectfully and safely.
