We've made it simple to report an animal that needs to be picked up. You have options:

  1. Call our toll-free number: 1-888-555-5555

  2. Use the reporting form and map:
    • Submit a report form with as much detail as possible.
    • Upon form submission, a marker will be placed on the map to mark the pickup site, and our team will be notified.
