How to Report a Roadside Animal

We've made it simple to report an animal that needs to be picked up. You have options:

  1. Call our toll-free number: 1-888-555-5555
    • Leave a message with the address and location.
    • Provide the type of animal and a brief description.
    • Mention if the animal is in the middle or side of the road.
  2. Use the reporting form or map:
    • Fill out the form on the next page with as much detail as possible.
    • Or, use the map to drop a pin on the animal's location and select a description from the available options.
