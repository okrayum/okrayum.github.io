City Services - About the Animal Report App

Streamlining city services for a cleaner, safer community.

Our Vision

The Animal Report App was created to address a common problem in urban environments: the challenge of reporting issues that require city intervention. Our mission is to provide residents with an easy-to-use tool to ensure city services can address issues quickly and effectively.

How It Works

This app simplifies the reporting process by allowing users to:

  • Fill out a form to provide detailed information about the issue.
  • See the reported location marked on a Google Map for easy reference.
  • View a list of open reports for transparency and follow-up.

Currently, we focus on reporting deceased animals needing pick-up on city property, but future plans include expanding the app to report potholes, broken streetlights, abandoned vehicles, and more.

Future Enhancements

Our goal is to make the reporting process even more seamless. Planned updates include:

  • Allowing users to place markers directly on the map to specify locations.
  • Adding features for reporting various city maintenance needs in a single app.
  • Collaborating with city departments to ensure reports are routed efficiently to the appropriate teams.

By making reporting easier, we hope to improve city upkeep and contribute to safer, cleaner communities.
